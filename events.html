Celebrated: A Royal Priesthood

Father's Day


Join us in honoring and celebrating the fathers and father figures in our community at our special Father’s Day event! This day is dedicated to recognizing the love, dedication, and hard work of all the dads who play a vital role in our families and church. We invite everyone to come together for an afternoon filled with joy, appreciation, and fellowship.

Our approach is holistic, addressing the spiritual, emotional, and social needs of children. We believe that learning about God should be engaging and interactive, which is why our programs are filled with hands-on activities, worship, and Bible stories that bring Scripture to life. We strive to make church a place where children are eager to come, learn, and participate.

We also place a strong emphasis on building a supportive community for both children and their families. Through our various events, such as family worship nights, community service projects, and parent workshops, we create opportunities for families to grow together in their faith journey.



Congrulations class of 2020

This is just the beginning, Never Stop Learning


We are thrilled to celebrate and honor the incredible achievements of our 2020 graduates! Despite the unprecedented challenges and uncertainties faced during their final year, these outstanding individuals have demonstrated remarkable resilience, determination, and faith. We are immensely proud of each graduate and are excited to see how God will continue to work in their lives as they embark on new journeys.

Dear Graduates, Congratulations on reaching this significant milestone in your life! Completing your education is a tremendous accomplishment, and we commend you for your hard work, perseverance, and dedication. As you step into this new chapter, we want to remind you that your church family is here to support you and celebrate your achievements.

As a token of our appreciation and recognition, each graduate will receive a special gift from the church. This gift is a small reminder of our love and support for you as you move forward into the future.
